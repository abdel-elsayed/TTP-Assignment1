Why Care About The Ocean?

The Ocean produces over half of the world's oxygen and absorbs 50 times more carbon dioxide than the atmosphere. It helps regulate our climate and weather since it covers 70% of the Earth's surface, and 76% of all U.S. trade involves some sort of marine transportation

Overfishing

Put succinctly, we are fishing too much. We have removed two-thirds of the large fish in the ocean and have destroyed ocean ecosystems across the globe. This is resulting in a growing threat for food security for millions of people.

Global Warming

Global warming is resulting in rising sea levels which are threatening any and all populations located near the coast.

Pesticides

Many pesticides used end up going into coastal waters which result in oxygen depletion in the water. This in turn kills both marine plants and shellfish.
